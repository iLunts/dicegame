About Us

About Us

DICEGAME is a decentralized platform for hosting fair P2P games with transparent and verifiable mechanics, with no limitation on deposit and withdrawal. The platform is based on Graphene technology which allows to have low fees and high transaction speed.

Features

Say goodbye to Random Number Generator

Users play with each other, but not against the platform. DICEGAME is just the initiator and organizator of a game.

No special skills required to increase winning odds

Dice roll is the only task to be solved which does not require any particular knowledge or strategy. Just do the rolling and blockchain will take care of all other calculations and estimations.

High level of security

Do you have a way to change the data in more than 50K ledgers when there are 100K of them in the P2P network? Definetely, the answer is "no". But, in case there is a minor chance to do this, then does it really make sense at all? Thus, with the help of the blockchain we make it evident that the architecture of decentralized ledgers makes it almost impossible to lose funds or private information to cybercriminals.

High speed transactions

We use Graphene in the DICEGAME. This choice is proved by the ability of this technology to do things in parallel and speed up the transactions. Just imagine, Graphene has achived 10,000-100,000 transactions per second in stress tests. Thus, DICEGAME is the integration of all these attributes: speed, parallelization and scalability.

Face-to-face games in the peer-to-peer network

Games are conducted against real players, so that all participants have a fifty-fifty chance to win. It’s a basic premise for all our games. Thus, in the one-to-one game winning probability for each participant is 50% while in the sessions with higher number of players odds of winning depends on rules of the certain game ortournament, but in the meantime it meets the principles mentioned above.

Deposits and withdrawals at user's disposal

We do not impose limits on deposits and withdrawals. We think that the user should keep control over his own money.

Bonus tournament

A half of the company’s revenue will be played out in the bonus tournaments on a regular basis. Solid investors get life-long benefits participating in all bonus tournaments without fulfilment of any conditions.

How it Works

The technical implementation provides compliance with the principles and values that are common for all games based on the DICEGAME platform. It offers such advantages as transparency and simple ways to verify results. All algorithms from game mechanics to tournaments rely extensively on smart contracts. In addition to that, blockchain also allows to have reliable and secure betting and awarding mechanisms.

There are two main values that we were holding in mind while selecting the appropriate algorithm: having no reason to cheat and having a simple way to verify game results. To get more information about inner workings of the DICEGAME platform, please check out our video "How it works?"

Dice Tokens

Dice Tokens

The Dice token is a gaming chip that is used by DICEGAME platform. It can be exchanged to and from popular cryptocurrency using the integrated Dice Wallet.

Key benefits of Dice tokens:

  • Lower fee when using Dice tokens in game;
  • High transaction speed;
  • 100% transparent transactions;
  • Possibility to convert the Dice token into other cryptocurrency using the Dice Wallet integrated into our platform;
Economy

Tokens Distribution

Our primary goal is to make DICEGAME platform accessible around the globe and allow people to play our games anywhere conveniently. Upon reaching soft cap we will be able to cover rather small local market. With larger investment we are going to take a share in global gambling.

Raised Fund

All operations and interactions between investors and DICEGAME platform are conducted by smart contracts. They define all the logic and conditions that are impossible to change. Also we provide one extra advantage to Dice token holders: if at the end of sale period the coins used to buy our token have raised in price, we will compensate for this by transferring additional tokens. It allows to invest without worrying about current exchange rates.

Bonus Tournaments

Regular bonus tournaments is an extremely important concept of the project. Every new tournament or one-on-one game contributes half of its fee towards the prize fund of the BT. The very first BT on the DICEGAME platform will have a prize fund equivalent to $1kk. No bet is needed to participate so there is absolutely no financial risk.

Ways to get access to bonus tournaments (BT):

  • People can exchange nominal chips for the BT ticket. Nominal chips can be earned on daily basis by playing any games presented on the platform.
  • There is a chance to gain a BT ticket in the free-roll tournament.
  • The BT ticket can be given upon registration or some promo event.
  • The BT ticket can be awarded by the platform as a result of completing some quests.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the DICEGAME?

DICEGAME is a decentralized iGaming platform based on Graphene blockchain. It provides fair and transparent place for hosting P2P games.

What are the features of DICEGAME platform that make it unique?

Regular BT (bonus tournaments), absence of centralized RNG (random number generator), only face-to-face games (no bank nor casino involved), high level of security, high transaction speeds, low transaction fees, unlimited deposits and withdrawals.

Why Graphene?

Graphene blockchain is highly suitable because it offers freedom for customization and allows for low transaction fees and high transaction speeds.

Tell us about Dice token

For the ICO there will be 400 000 000 (Hard Cap) of the Dice tokens issued. Dice token offers a number of benefits:
- lower fees for games and transactions;
- high transaction speed;
- is interchangeable with popular coins;
- contributes to the BT ticket acquisition and helps platform development
