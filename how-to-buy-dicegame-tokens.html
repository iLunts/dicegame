INSTRUCTION HOW TO BUY DICEGAME TOKENS DIRECTLY FROM SMART CONTRACT

Whenever you wish to send ETH to crowdsale smart contract it is strongly recommended to to verify its owner. This can be accomplished using https://www.clearify.io service.

Our official smart contract address:
0x30B149e87Fc634FAaca4e46A807A04357B0816d7

After checking it with clearify, it should state that it belongs to DICEGAME https://dicegame.io

Step 1

Using your browser open link https://www.myetherwallet.com/#contracts

Please make sure it opens correctly and you see following window:

Step 2

Copy DICEGAME smart contract address to «Contract Address» field:

Step 3

To obtain information for «ABI / JSON» field, you must open link:
https://etherscan.io/address/0x30B149e87Fc634FAaca4e46A807A04357B0816d7#code

And then past it to «ABI / JSON» field shown on page:
https://www.myetherwallet.com/#contracts

Step 4

After Step 2 and Step 3 will be completed, you must Access smart contract using «Access» button.

Step 5

After accessing smart contract «Read / Write Contract» dropdown menu will appear. Please find correct «buyTokens» function (which accepts a single parameter called 'beneficiary')

Step 6

After successful Step 5 you will see a picture like shown below

All you need to do now is «Unlock» your wallet using prefered method.

Step 7

Please provide beneficiary address (address to send ERC20 tokens to) and click «Write» button

Step 8

In new opened pop-up window:

You must fill «Amount to Send» in ETH and GAS Limit (in most cases 200.000 GAS should suffice) Then click «Generate Transaction» button.

Step 9

Please carefully check provided information. If everything is correct then click «Yes, I am sure! Make transaсtion.» The following badge informs that all went good:
